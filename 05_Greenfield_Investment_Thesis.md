## CORE THESIS

> **Commit ₹800–1,200 crore equity into a 75–120 acre integrated township in NCR's eastern corridor (Indirapuram/Crossing Republik/Greater Noida) — a mixed-use, "city-within-a-city" development targeting 8–12 million sqft of saleable area across residential, retail, office, and hospitality, capturing the Prestige-style township premium and delivering 22–28% gross IRR through phased development, price discovery, and asset class diversification.**

---

## WHY NOW

### Macro Tailwinds
- **Township demand is structural:** India's top-15 townships (Lodha Palava, Prestige City, Godrej Garden City) have outperformed standalone projects by 30–50% on sales velocity — consumers prefer integrated ecosystems
- **NCR eastern corridor underserved:** Ghaziabad-Noida corridor has 25M+ population but zero institutional-grade integrated townships — the gap is glaring
- **Policy support:** NCR Regional Plan 2041 designates eastern corridor for 'self-sufficient urban nodes'; UP government incentivizing large-format development (25% FSI bonus for townships >25 acres)
- **Credit environment:** 8.25–8.75% home loan rates; 15-year high in mortgage disbursements (₹9.5 lakh crore outstanding)
- **Work-from-anywhere:** Hybrid work has accelerated demand for walkable, amenity-rich communities — townships over tower-only projects

### NCR-Specific Catalysts
- **Jewar Airport (40 km):** Phase 1 opening Dec 2027 — transformational for eastern NCR land values
- **RRTS Meerut (operational):** Ghaziabad connected to Delhi in 38 minutes
- **Noida-Greater Noida Metro extension:** Under construction — enhances connectivity
- **Eastern Peripheral Expressway:** Fully operational — logistics and industrial demand driving ancillary residential
- **IT/ITeS migration:** Noida Sector 62/135/142 office corridor expanding; 15M+ sqft under development — captive residential demand

## KEY VALUE DRIVERS

| Driver | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Land at ₹1,500–2,500/sqft** (75–120 acres) vs. ₹5,000–10,000/sqft for smaller infill parcels | Only large-format buyers can access these parcels — natural moat |
| **Phased development over 7–10 years** — price discovery across phases | Phase 1 at ₹5,500/sqft → Phase 4 at ₹9,000–11,000/sqft (60–100% appreciation) |
| **Mixed-use diversification** — residential (60%), retail (15%), office (15%), hospitality (10%) | Reduces single-segment risk; commercial component generates recurring income |
| **Township premium** — established townships command 15–25% premium to nearby standalone | Brand + amenity + community effect |
| **Amenity-driven appreciation** — clubhouse, school, hospital, retail mall delivered in early phases | Drives Phase 2–4 pricing power |
| **FSI optimization** — UP township policy allows 3.5–4.0 FSI (vs. 2.5 for standalone) | 40–60% more saleable area per acre |

## EDGE / COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE

1. **Scale barrier to entry:** 75–120 acres requires ₹200–400 crore land investment — eliminates 95% of local developers. Only institutional capital can underwrite
2. **Self-reinforcing ecosystem:** Each delivered phase enhances value of subsequent phases — gym, school, retail create a flywheel effect
3. **Brand premium in unbranded corridor:** Prestige/Godrej/Lodha-style product in a corridor with no institutional township → category creation
4. **Commercial component as collateral:** 2–3M sqft of office + retail generates ₹80–120 crore NOI at stabilization — provides downside protection and REIT optionality
5. **Multi-cycle resilience:** 7–10 year development timeline spans market cycles — phases can accelerate or decelerate based on demand

## KEY RISKS

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|
| **Execution complexity** — large-scale infrastructure (roads, STP, power), multi-phase permitting, 7–10 year timeline | HIGH | Proven township developer partner (Prestige, Godrej, Lodha); phased master plan with independent completion per phase; robust PMC oversight |
| **Demand risk in outer NCR** — end-user demand in Indirapuram/Crossing Republik corridor is price-sensitive and cyclical | MEDIUM | Phase 1 focused on ₹40–65 lakh units (deepest demand); 50% pre-sales gate before next phase launch |
| **Regulatory/land title risk** — agricultural land conversion, encumbrance, litigation (common in UP) | HIGH | Full legal DD; title insurance; avoid government-allotted contested parcels; preference for consolidated single-owner land |
