---

## CORE THESIS

> **Deploy ₹600 Cr equity into a JDA-structured mid-income residential platform across 3 land parcels (50 acres) in Sonipat–Panipat corridor, generating 5.2M sqft saleable area at ₹4,000–5,500/sqft ASP against ₹2,200/sqft construction cost — yielding 58% gross margin, 22.4% unlevered project IRR, 28.6% levered developer IRR, and 2.15x MOIC over 20 quarters (5 years).**

## WHY NOW

### NCR Affordability + Infrastructure Convergence
- **Price correction complete:** Real prices in Sonipat/Panipat at 2015 levels; floor established after 7-year correction
- **Delhi–Sonipat RRTS (approved, ₹6,400 Cr):** Sonipat → Delhi in 25 min by 2029 — transformational
- **KMP Expressway (operational):** Connects Sonipat to Gurugram/Manesar industrial belt
- **NH-44 widening (8-lane):** Delhi–Panipat reduced to 90 min
- **Jewar Airport (Phase 1: Dec 2027):** Rising tide for all NCR periphery
- **Home loan rates at 8.25–8.75%:** 3-year low — peak affordability window
- **PMAY extension:** ₹2.67L subsidy for ₹25–45L homes — our exact target segment

### Market Structure Favors Institutional Entry
- **RERA cleanup:** 60%+ unorganized Sonipat/Panipat developers exited post-2018
- **₹50,000 Cr annual funding gap** for mid-tier NCR developers
- **Zero institutional residential PE** in Sonipat/Panipat — first mover advantage
- **End-user driven demand (85%+):** Govt employees, textile workers, SME — low cancellation risk
