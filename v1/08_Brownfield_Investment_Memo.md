## 2. INVESTMENT THESIS

### Core Thesis
NCR's mid-tier developers sit on ₹15,000–20,000 crore of partially completed inventory that needs ₹5,000–8,000 crore of completion capital. They can't access it — banks won't lend to sub-₹2,000 Cr revenue developers, NBFCs have retreated, and RERA escrow locks 70% of collections. We provide the last-mile capital at 18–22% preferred return + profit share, secured by project assets and personal guarantees, with 30–36 month payback.

## 6. SENSITIVITY ANALYSIS

### 6.1 What Breaks the Deal?

| Variable | Base | Stress | IRR Impact | Breaker? |
|----------|------|--------|-----------|----------|
| ASP decline 15% | 6,400 | 5,440 | IRR → 14% | ⚠️ Below hurdle |
| Velocity -40% | 20 u/mo | 12 u/mo | IRR → 14%, payback 48mo | ⚠️ Duration risk |

| Developer default (1 of 6 projects) | 0 | 1 total loss | IRR → 16% | No — portfolio diversified |
| All projects delayed 12 months | On time | +12 mo | IRR → 16% | No |

### 6.2 Scenario Matrix — Net IRR

| | Velocity -20% | Velocity Base | Velocity +20% |
|---|-------------|--------------|---------------|
| **ASP -10%** | 13% | 16% | 19% |
| **ASP Base** | 16% | 20% | 24% |
| **ASP +10%** | 20% | 24% | 28% |

### 6.3 Key Insight
- **Portfolio effect is powerful:** Even if 1 of 6 projects is a total write-off, portfolio IRR stays at 16%
- **Preferred return provides cushion:** 18% pref means fund gets paid before developer in any scenario
- **Duration is the primary sensitivity** — delays kill IRR more than price declines

### Key Operating Principles
1. **Never deploy >30% of project equity in first tranche** — remaining tied to milestones
2. **Monthly construction progress verification** (independent engineer)
3. **Weekly bank statement monitoring** — detect cash flow anomalies within 7 days
4. **Quarterly forensic audit** — related party, cost benchmarking, vendor verification
5. **Sales velocity tracking** — if velocity falls below 60% of plan for 2 consecutive months, trigger review

## 9. EXIT STRATEGY

### Primary: Project Sell-Down (75%)
- Capital returned through project collections as units are sold and delivered
- Preferred return (18–20%) paid first from project cash flows
- Profit share distributed after full preferred return recovery
- Timeline: 30–42 months per project

### Secondary: Developer Buyback (15%)
- Put option at 18% IRR compounding, exercisable at M36–M48
- Secured by personal guarantees + pledged assets
- Used if market conditions deteriorate or slow velocity persists

### Tertiary: Secondary Sale (5%)
- Sell structured instrument to another PE fund
- Discount of 200–300bps to face value for liquidity
- Used only for portfolio rebalancing or early exit needs

### Last Resort: Collateral Enforcement (5%)
- Enforce charge on unsold inventory + land
- Liquidate through distressed asset channels
- Recovery: 60–80% of outstanding (based on 1.3x collateral coverage)
