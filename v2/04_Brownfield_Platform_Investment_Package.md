## CORE THESIS

> **Deploy ₹400 Cr structured equity across 5 mid-stage NCR residential projects (30–65% complete) via milestone-linked capital infusion — accelerating construction from 48-month to 30-month completion, unlocking 25–35% completion premium on unsold inventory, and generating 20% net IRR / 1.9x MOIC in 3.2 years with the lowest execution risk and shortest duration in the portfolio.**

---

## WHY NOW

- **₹50,000 Cr annual funding gap:** Post-NBFC crisis, mid-tier NCR developers (₹300–1,500 Cr revenue) cannot access affordable debt — PE fills the void at premium terms
- **Completion = value creation:** NCR buyers pay 25–35% premium for OC-ready vs. under-construction; capital injection that accelerates OC directly creates value
- **RERA escrow discipline:** 70% of collections must fund construction — capital infusion is the only way to break the cash flow logjam
- **NCR trust deficit is our moat:** After Jaypee/Unitech/Amrapali, NCR buyers ONLY buy from developers showing visible construction progress — our capital = their construction = their sales
