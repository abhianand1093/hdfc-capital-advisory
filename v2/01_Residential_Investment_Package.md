# SECTION 1: INVESTMENT THESIS

---

## CORE THESIS

> **Deploy ₹500–700 Cr equity via a scalable mid-income residential platform in NCR spillover markets (Sonipat, Panipat, Sohna) where land at ₹1.0–1.5 Cr/acre, construction at ₹1,800–2,200/sqft, and ASP of ₹4,000–5,500/sqft yield 55–65% gross margins, 60–70% pre-sales de-risk capital, and infrastructure catalysts provide free price optionality — targeting 20%+ net IRR and 2.0x MOIC over 5–6 years.**

---

## WHY NOW

### NCR Affordability Crisis Creates Spillover Demand
- Gurugram/Noida core: ₹12,000–18,000/sqft → priced out for ₹6–12 LPA households
- Sonipat/Panipat: ₹3,500–5,500/sqft → 3–4x more affordable, 45–60 min commute via NH-44
- Target buyer: Government employees, SME workers, teachers, healthcare — stable income, low default

### Infrastructure Is the Catalyst
- **Delhi–Sonipat RRTS** (approved, ₹6,400 Cr): Sonipat → Delhi in 25 min by 2029
- **KMP Expressway** (operational): Connects Sonipat to Gurugram/Manesar industrial belt
- **Jewar Airport** (Phase 1: Dec 2027): Eastern NCR anchor — rising tide for all NCR periphery
- **NH-44 widening** (Panipat section): 6-lane → 8-lane, reducing Delhi–Panipat to 90 min

### Post-RERA Market Consolidation
- 60%+ unorganized Sonipat/Panipat developers exited post-2018
- Surviving top-5 control 70% of new launches — oligopoly pricing power
- Institutional capital is absent — first-mover advantage is real
